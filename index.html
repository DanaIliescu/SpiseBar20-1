About

Things to share. Small plates. Salads and sandwiches. Camparisoda. The bar at Luca is a separate space, open all day, for espresso and pastries from 8am, a salad or a sandwich for lunch and a menu of small plates at dinnertime. You are welcome throughout the day at any time. From now until Christmas the bar will open at midday. From mid January it will be open from 8am.

Menu

We have the most amazing menu with a variety of excellent food, a blody tasty wine selection and sweet yet powerful cocktails. The menu is changed every week, providing fresh new products every time, ready to tickle your soul.
